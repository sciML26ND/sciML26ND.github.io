Information

September 26 – 27, 2026  ·  University of Notre Dame

Registration

Please register before Friday September 18, 2026 if you will be attending. There is no charge for registration, but registration is required for an accurate lunch count.

Abstract Submission

We will also have a poster session and lightning talks for students or junior researchers who are interested in sharing their work. Please submit your title, abstract and the presentation format HERE before September 4, 2026.

Getting to Notre Dame

The workshop will be held at the University of Notre Dame, Notre Dame, Indiana 46556. Notre Dame is located just north of South Bend, IN, approximately 90 miles east of Chicago.

✈️
By Air — South Bend (SBN)

South Bend International Airport (SBN) is the closest airport, ~10 miles from campus (~15 min by car).

Direct connections from Chicago O'Hare, Detroit, Charlotte, New York, Philadelphia, and Washington D.C.

sbnair.com

✈️
By Air — Chicago O'Hare (ORD)

Chicago O'Hare (ORD) offers the most flight options. Notre Dame is ~90 miles east (~1 h 45 min by car).

Alternatively, the South Shore Line commuter rail connects Chicago to South Bend (~2.5 hours).

mysouthshoreline.com

🚗
By Car

Accessible via the Indiana Toll Road (I-80/I-90). Take exit 77 (IN-933) north toward Notre Dame.

Visitor parking info: parking.nd.edu

🚆
By Train

Amtrak serves South Bend via Chicago Union Station (Capitol Limited, Lake Shore Limited). The station is ~5 miles from campus.

amtrak.com

🏨
Accommodation

The Morris Inn on campus is the closest option. More hotels in nearby Mishawaka and South Bend.
